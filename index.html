👋 About Us

We're on a mission to make
clinical research careers accessible to everyone

TrialPath was born from a simple frustration — great clinical research talent was going untapped because quality, affordable, industry-aligned training simply didn't exist. We decided to change that.

🧬

Our Mission

Bridging the gap between education and industry

Clinical Data Management is one of the fastest-growing fields in pharmaceutical research — yet most graduates have never heard of it. TrialPath changes that by bringing real industry workflows, live EDC tool practice, and mentor-backed learning directly to your screen.

We work with seasoned CDM professionals, regulatory experts, and biotech leaders to ensure every lesson reflects what's actually happening in trials today — not outdated textbook theory.

Whether you're a fresh graduate figuring out your first step, or a CRA looking to transition into data management — TrialPath was built for you. 🙌

Our Journey

From a small idea to a global platform

2019
TrialPath is founded 🚀
Ananya launches the first CDM fundamentals course after seeing dozens of talented graduates unable to break into clinical research.
2020
First 500 students enrolled
Word spreads fast. TrialPath hits 500 enrolled learners within 6 months and launches live instructor-led batches for the first time.
2021
EDC Hands-on Lab launched 💻
Partnership with simulation technology providers enables the first-ever hands-on Medidata Rave and Oracle Inform practice environment for online learners.
2023
10,000 learners milestone 🎉
TrialPath crosses 10,000 enrolled learners across 12 countries. Corporate training division launched serving CROs and pharma companies.
2025
12,000+ strong & growing 🌍
120+ courses, 40+ instructors, 15 countries. TrialPath is now the most trusted CDM training platform in the US and growing globally.
